Fairway Fix Golf is Lake Highland's premier indoor golf simulator club.

  • Dual swing suites (with room to grow)
  • Trackman Simulators w/ 400+ courses
  • 24/7/365 facility access
  • Climate controlled (new ACs)
  • Monthly challenges and leagues
  • BYO Food + Drink

MEMBERSHIPS

Membership is limited to 40 golfers per bay to allow for booking availability. Walk-ins are welcome between 10am-5pm if there is availability within 3 hours of the requested time slot. Members can book 6 months in advance.

Single 10 hours/month + 1 free guest 2 bookings scheduled at a time $150/month
Family/Super 15 hours/month + 3 free guests 3 bookings scheduled at a time $250/month
Walk-In (Limited) 1 hour min/max + 20/add'l player Book 3 hours in advance $30/hour
Corporate & Small Business Memberships Parties & Events Email adam@fairwayfixdallas.com

HOW IT WORKS

  1. Click on Join the Club to sign up for a monthly membership or Book 1 Hour to rent for an hour.
  2. Enter your personal and credit card info.
  3. Select the date, time and bay for your booking
  4. 15 minutes before your booking you will receive a text/email with your personal door codes.
  5. Log in to your bay, choose your course and play!
  6. Once play begins a timer will start counting down your remaining time.
